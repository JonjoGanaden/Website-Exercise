About this tea

If you're not familiar with matcha, it's a Japanese green tea powder made from finely powdered dried tea leaves. It has a slightly bitter, vegetal taste and a vibrant green color that results from the leaves' high chlorophyll levels.

Ingredients

  • Green tea leaves
